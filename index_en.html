Welcome to the official website of the International Anti-Criminal and Anti-Terrorist Subcommittee of the Lviv Region. Our committee provides coordination and cooperation in the fight against criminal and terrorist activities to ensure the security of our region and the international community.

About Us

The International Anti-Criminal and Anti-Terrorist Subcommittee of the Lviv Region is an organization that unites highly qualified specialists in the field of combating crime and terrorism. Our mission is to ensure the safety of citizens and strengthen the legal order through effective cooperation and innovative approaches.

Team

Our committee is made up of highly qualified and dedicated professionals who have many years of experience in the fight against crime and terrorism. Every member of our team works for the benefit of the general welfare and safety of all.

"Magnetic storms and Road Safety: Initiative of Lubomir Sopilnyk"

In the new stage of patrolling the roads of Lviv Oblast, an unexpected alliance appears - scientist and law enforcement officer Lyubomyr Sopilnyk decided to investigate the impact of magnetic storms on road safety. The head of the Scientific Center of the Ministry of Internal Affairs and Communications of Ukraine in the Lviv region proved that these natural phenomena can significantly increase the risk of traffic accidents. According to the results of his research, it was found that during magnetic storms there is an increase in the reaction time of drivers, changes in temperature and an increase or decrease in cranial pressure. Based on his research, Sopilnyk developed warning signs that can reduce the risk of accidents during magnetic storms.

The anti-accident technique of Colonel Sopilnyk, which is used in Poland, will be tested in the Lviv Region

With the support of the leadership of the State Security Service of Ukraine in the Lviv region, Lyubomyr Sopilnyk is implementing new security measures. According to the order, police and traffic police units will work in an enhanced mode during magnetic storms. The article reveals the details of the initiative and the scientific research of Lubomir Sopilnyk, and also describes the expected positive results of the implementation of these measures to reduce accidents and crime on the roads. It emphasizes its commitment to ensure public safety and improve the quality of traffic during natural phenomena.
